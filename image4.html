  • Arnold Schwarzenegger: just an hard worker

    22 May 2016 admin 0 comment

    The beginning

    Arnold Schwarzenegger was born July 30, 1947 in Tahl, an Austrian village of about 1200 inhabitants. He grew up in the second floor of an old house with his parents Gustav and Aurelia and his older brother Meinhard. The family is very poor, so that for several years, although his father a police officer, live in very serious financial difficulties, to the point of not even have a refrigerator to store food. As a boy, however, he practices many sports such as football, athletics, boxing and javelin.

    Soon, however, he understands that, in general, group sports do not excite much but that is more attracted to something that will enable him to develop his personality and his talents independently, that is, through a personal program, away from any form of competition.

    An icon of bodybuilding

    The natural outcome of this approach and this conception takes him to be interested in body-building, which occurs immediately in his eyes like the perfect synthesis of what he sought. Having said that, he enrolled a gym and begins to lifts the firsts weights.

    With the passage of time, and with the changing of requirements, he relies on a coach, not only to perfect the exercises, but also to make the technical leap that alone is not able to fulfill. In a few time, the results of these great efforts are beginning to be visible: take form that enormous muscular development and shape for what Schwarzenegger will be known.

    As soon as he is dismissed from military service, he try the competitions road. It ‘a success on the first try. Vince immediately the title of Mr. Europe Jr. and, soon after, at the age of 20 years is named Mr. Europe. Not only that, beating all competitors is also elected Mr. Universe (plus competing with his idol, Reg Park). The organizers just did two calculations to understand, among other things, that it is the youngest of the haloed history of the award. In this period he also knows Franco Columbu, who would become from then on his inseparable friend of training and life.

    The American Dream

    Later, he faces other competitions, all resolved in his favor. We can therefore say that at that time the young athlete has already built an image, which has become a true character, and Arnold knows this. He decide to have success in entertainment and try to access that golden world using just that role, even small, that he have for the media. He arrived in America in 1968. Here he won his first title of Mr. Olympia, the promotion that opens him the door of the first set, for the movie “Hercules in New York” in 1971.

    After winning his sixth title of Mr. Olympia in 1975, Arnold retires from body-building. Also, few people know that Schwarzenegger is not only the macho all muscle but no brains, indeed in November 1979, he graduated in economics with a specialization in marketing fitness physicist at the University of Wisconsin, redeeming in this way, not only with professional success, a life of poverty and toil. In 1980, he returned to competitions and he won his seventh Mr. Olympia title.

    Three years later, September 9, 1983 Schwarzenegger became a US citizen. 1985 is the year in which he was elected international star of the year. On June 2, 1987, now an international character thanks to the numerous movies credits, is given to him the star on the famous “Hollywood’s Walk of Fame”.

    But Schwarzenegger will not forget the old love for the sport that allowed him to get where he is. In 1989, therefore, he launched the “Arnold Classic”, one of the most important bodybuilding tournaments.

    The political career

    In the subsequent years Schwarzenegger runs many other films. In April 16, 1997 the actor was forced to undergo a surgery to the heart, a very delicate operation, which fortunately manages without further complications. 2001 marks an important step in the life of Schwarzenegger, divided between a possible political career or the continuation of that of actor.

    In the aftermath of the release of the third chapter of the Terminator saga, Arnold, candidate for Governor of California to the elections is elected in October 7, 2003, following the footsteps of Ronald Reagan, also came from Hollywood and from the direction of the California state before coming to the US presidency. Schwarzenegger being born in Austria, however, can not aspire to the charge of President. Three years later, in the month of November 2006 he has got the re-election. He also dedicated himself to promoting physical education and after-school programs, and continues to commit his time, energy and personal finances to charitable organizations around the world.
